About this page

The materials in this page are intended to support the dissertation titled "A map of Visual Literacy", presented by Ernesto Peña in partial fulfillment of the requirements to obtain the degree of Ph.D. in Language and Literacy Education.

All the items listed here are referred from Chapter 4.

Early attempts of interactive visualization

The first attempts to create what was later known as the Glass Cast included an interactive visualization generated directly from Gephi. This visualization employs sigma.js and, although is fully operational, it was never intended to be used as a final product.

Zotero report

All the documents used for populating the Glass Cast are listed in a HTML document produced through Zotero. This document, known as "Zotero Report" can be downloaded and used to produce the Glass Cast visualization.
