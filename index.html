About this page

The materials in this page are intended to support the dissertation titled Visualizing Visual Literacy, presented by Ernesto Peña in partial fulfillment of the requirements to obtain the degree of Ph.D. in Language and Literacy Education.
All the items listed here are referred from Chapter 4: A Three-Dimensional Map of Visual Literacy.

Note: Although this website is responsive and can be seen in mobile devices, all of the interfaces linked from it require a desktop computer for their correct visualization.

Early attempts of interactive visualization

The first attempts to create what was later known as the Glass Cast included an interactive visualization generated directly from Gephi. This visualization employs sigma.js and, although is fully operational, it was never intended to be used as a final product.
